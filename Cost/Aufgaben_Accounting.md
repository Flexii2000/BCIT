## Accounting-Formula-Sheet
[Table of Contents](#Table-of-Contents)

### 1. Basic Contribution Margin Formulas
CM per unit = $P - V$  
Total CM = $(P - V) \times Q$

---

### 2. Fixed Cost and Break-Even
Break-even (units) = $\frac{Fixed\ Costs}{CM_{unit}}$  
Break-even (sales $) = $BEP_{units} \times P$

---

### 3. Target Profit (Before Taxes)
If target operating income is given:  
$Q = \frac{Fixed\ Costs + Target\ Operating\ Income}{CM_{unit}}$

If target net income (after tax) is given:  
$Target\ Operating\ Income = \frac{Target\ Net\ Income}{1 - Tax\ Rate}$  

Then:  
$Q = \frac{Fixed\ Costs + Target\ Operating\ Income}{CM_{unit}}$

---

### 4. Contribution Margin Income Statement
Total Revenue = $P \times Q$  
Total Variable Cost = $V_{unit} \times Q$  
Contribution Margin = $Revenue - Variable\ Costs$  
Operating Income (Loss) = $Contribution\ Margin - Fixed\ Costs$

---

### 5. Commission and Multi-Revenue Adjustments
If revenue is a percentage commission:  
$P_{effective} = P_{ticket} \times Commission\ Rate$

If revenue has multiple components (e.g., commission + service fee):  
$P_{effective} = P_{commission} + P_{fee}$  
$V_{effective} = V_{ticket} + V_{fee}$

---

### 6. Operating Leverage and Mixed Cost Structure
Operating income = $(P - V) \times Q - F$

If cost is a % of revenue, convert it to an equivalent variable cost rate:  
$V_{effective} = V + (Commission\% \times P)$

Breakeven (units) = $\frac{F}{P - V}$

Degree of Operating Leverage (DOL) = $\frac{CM}{Operating\ Income}$  
or equivalently  
$DOL = \frac{(P - V) \times Q}{(P - V) \times Q - F}$

---

### 7. Margin of Safety (MOS)
Margin of Safety (units) = $Actual\ Sales\ Units - BEP_{units}$  
Margin of Safety (ratio) = $\frac{Actual\ Sales - BE\ Sales}{Actual\ Sales}$  

or equivalently = $MOS\% = 1 - \frac{BE\ Units}{Actual\ Units}$

---

### 8. Multi-Product CVP Analysis
Weighted-Average CM per composite unit =  
$\frac{\sum (CM_i \times Sales\ Mix_i)}{\sum Sales\ Mix_i}$

Break-even (composite units) = $\frac{Fixed\ Costs}{WACM}$  
Break-even for each product = $BEP_{composite} \times Sales\ Mix_i$

Break-even (sales $) = \sum (BEP_i \times P_i)$
